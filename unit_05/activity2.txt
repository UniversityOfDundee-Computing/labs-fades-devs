Changes over time (1998 to 2025):
- More focus on white space (margins and paddings)
- Minimalistic design
- Subtle shadows
- Use of sans-serif fonts
- Focus on the overall aesthetic (not only the functionality)
- Leverage of menus to hide content in different sections
- More use of rounded corners
- Increase of font sizes for better readability
